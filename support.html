Small Claims Evidence Binder Support

Small Claims Evidence Binder is a validation concept for a local-first iOS evidence organization app.

Planned Workflow

  • Create one local dispute packet.
  • Add timeline rows and evidence rows.
  • Number exhibits and link evidence to events.
  • Review missing source-file, date, and exchange-note gaps.
  • Preview a local Markdown or CSV packet.

Boundary

The planned app would organize user-entered records only. It does not provide legal advice, court filing, court upload, certified evidence, deadline calculation, damages calculation, attorney review, or outcome guarantees.

Small Claims Evidence Binder Privacy Policy

Small Claims Evidence Binder is currently a product validation concept, not a released app.

The planned app is local-first. It would store case notes, exhibit metadata, timeline rows, source file references, and generated exports on the user's device. It would not send dispute records, evidence files, photos, messages, receipts, court notes, or generated packets to the developer.

The planned app would not require an account, self-hosted server, court portal login, legal-service login, payment account login, or third-party legal API for the core workflow.

If you email validation feedback, send only product-interest feedback. Do not email private dispute records, evidence files, photos, receipts, messages, or legal documents during this probe.

This product concept does not provide legal advice, court filing, court upload, certified records, deadline calculation, damages calculation, attorney review, or outcome guarantees.
